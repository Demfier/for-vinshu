Dear Vinshu
& Sachin

⸻ ❦ ⸻

I wish more than anything that I could be there today — to hug you, to dance with you, to see you get married.

Vinshu — Watching you become someone's whole world is only surprising to the people who don't already know you. I've known you since childhood and I've been quietly watching you become extraordinary the whole time.

Sachin — our families don't really need introductions at this point. Glad it's you. See you both soon — properly, this time.

May your marriage be full of the big & small, ordinary & extraordinary moments of joy. May you be each other's biggest cheerleaders, truest readers, and safest places. May you love each other the way you both deserve, which is to say, completely.

God bless you both.

— your brother, Gaurav
