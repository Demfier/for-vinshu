Vinshu, my love

⸻ ❦ ⸻

I wish more than anything that I could be there today — to hug you, to dance with you, to cry a little when you walked out to the pheras. The world got in the way of the plane ticket, but not of this.

Watching you become someone's whole world is only surprising to the people who don't already know you. I've known you my entire life, and I've been quietly watching you become extraordinary the whole time.

May your marriage be full of the small, ordinary kinds of joy — bad jokes, shared chai, the fights you laugh about later. May he love you the way you deserve, which is to say, completely.

I love you. I love you. I love you.
No distance is long enough to change that.

— your sibling, always
